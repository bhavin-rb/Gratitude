Creating Positive Thoughts - 21 Days Challenge

Day 1

Practice Gratitude

Write 3 things you are grateful for every day, don't repeat points. Selecting unique points everyday helps you to focus on the positive rather than the negative.

Live Free!

Day 2

Journal

This encourages your brain to re-live good previous occasions which doubles the positive impact the situation had in your life. Everyday spend two minutes writing in detail about one positive experience that happened to you in the past 24 hours.

Live Free!

Day 3

Exercise

For 30 minutes engage in an exercise you enjoy like cycling, a run or walking. Morning and evening stretches are essential and when practices daily, they aid in the release of active hormones that sets active for the day and relaxes you in the evening. With exercise, you are training your body to preserve your happy factor.

Live Free!

Day 4

Meditate

Sit in a quiet place and focus on your breathing for at least 2 minutes a day. If you do this successfully it will help you sleep better, feel less stressed and have more energy. Quiet places can be: your balcony, a garden or an isolated room.

Live Free!

Day 5

Practice Acts of Kindness

Positive social connection is quite rejuvenating. These connections fuel your happiness factor. This can be simple such as writing an authentically positive and affirming email praising someone or thanking someone for his or her contributions in your life.

Live Free!

Day 6

Inspirational Materials

Spend some time each day reading something that encourages positive thinking. Read spiritual materials or inspirational quotes to help you focus on what's important to you in life. It can be a great way to start and end your day.

Live Free!

Day 7

Set Goals

Having goals keeps your mind active and sharp. Be clear what you want and set timelines. Read these goals over every day, it will strengthen the positive mindset you are building because you will have laser-sharp focus on what is important in your life.

Live Free!

Day 8

Clear Out Negative Thoughts

Power and Energy follows Thought! Which though do you nurture often? Think positively to act positively Those who say 'I can' and 'I will', will surely manage and those who say 'I cannot' will not manage. Your thoughts and your tongue have power to make things happen!

Live Free!

Day 9

Positive Words

Get a list of at least 20 positive words. Next, write down some of the words you use in a negative context frequently. Put a thin line through the center of each negative word. Over the crossed-out word, write your new word in bold letters. Your ego is limited but your true self is limitless, infinite! You can raise above your emotions!

Live Free!

Day 10

Self Love

Stand in front of the mirror for five minutes and focus on at least five things that you love about yourself. Write them down in your journal.

Live Free!

Day 11

Gratitude

How often do you pause to appreciate what you have in life? Make gratitude part of your everyday life. Gratitude activates positive emotions such as happiness and contentment and strengthens our neural pathways, ultimately making easier for ourselves to feel happy and positive.

Live Free!

Day 12

Act with Gratitude

Way to express gratitude:
Say thank you.
Tell some you love them.
Donate things you don't use.
Call a friend or family.
Compliment a stranger.
Thank yourself etc.

From the simple ones, move to the newest experiences you have that make you feel grateful.

Live Free!

Day 13

Help Someone

Today can be an exciting day for you, a way to build your gratitude and positive attitude. Help a needy people; could be stranger, a friend, neighbor or a family. You can make them feel human again, give them hope and this, in turn, should make you feel very positive and that you are a person of love and compassion.

Live Free!

Day 14

Diet

Poor diet plays a role in worsening mood disorders like anxiety and depression. Today, look closely how you been eating and decide what needs to be eliminated/added to your daily meals. Make sure to eat at least one fruit per day, vegetables and drink plenty of water.

Live Free!

Day 15

Listen to Music

Music can boost your mood, reduce stress and even improve sleep. When you listen to music, your brain and body functions start to slide and your train of thought slows down.

Live Free!

Day 16

Focus on The Good Things

When you encounter obstacles, focus on the benefits, no matter how small or seemingly insignificant they seem. If you look for it, you can always find the proverbial silver lining in every cloud even if it is not immediately obvious.

Live Free!

Day 17

Open Yourself up to Humor

Allow yourself to experience humor even in the darkest or most trying situations. No situation is permanent, give yourself permission to laugh, remind yourself that this situation will probably make for a good story later. It's not wrong to laugh when things are tough; on the contrary, it helps.

Live Free!

Day 18

Start Every Day on a Positive Note

How you start the morning sets the tone for the rest of the day. Start your day with positive affirmations. Talk to yourself in the mirror, even if you feel silly, with a statement like "Today is a good day". You will be amazed how much your day improves.

Live Free!

Day 19

Turn Failures into Lessons

Don't stress over the little things, you will experience failure in life. Instead of focusing on how you failed, think about what you are going to do next time, turn your failure into a lesson. It is the courage to continue that counts.

Live Free!

Day 20

Focus on The Present

Listen and be present. Often, we are trapped in thoughts of the future or the past that we forget to experience, let alone enjoy, what's happening right now. Relish or luxuriate in whatever you are doing at the present moment.

Live Free!

Day 21

Surround Yourself with Positive People

Positivity and Happiness are contagious When you surround yourself with positive people, you will hear positive outlooks, positive stories and positive thoughts. Remember that like attracts like. You have to give off positive vibes and be yourself to attract the people that are right for you.

Live Free!
